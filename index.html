Olashile Abayomi

A God driven entrepreneur, cook, philanthropist and public speaker

Olashile Abayomi is a young lady led divinely (Holy Spirit) to touch lives and provide solutions to the needs/problems of upwardly mobile people who generally love Homemade Nigerian Soups and Stews but are restricted due time constraints and this led to the birth of Soupamarket. It is suffice to say her passion met her calling..

She has in bag her BSc in Political Science and went ahead for her M.A in International Relations and world order.. She also bagged professional certifications in HSE and Oil and Gas Management.. She is also an Alumni of Enterprise Development Centre of The Pan Atlantic University.

Olashile uses her cooking talents to feed the underprivileged and she calls it #Soupastreetministry, she also travels to remote villages to empower orphans on how to make Ankara asscessories for free (a skill she learned when she was serving - NYSC) amongst her other charity works..

Her brand Soupamarket is a force to reckon with as she has worked with brands like Nestlé, Maggi, Cadbury, Google, Fan Ville, etc. She exports her soups internationally to countries like Spain, US, UK, Canada etc on demand.. She also produces other household over the shelf products like Soupa Ofada sauce oil, Soupa Pepper soup spice, Soupa Suya Spice, Soupa Blends, Soupa Hot Pepper Sauce

There is also Soupaeasy - This is solely to source for raw ingredients should the client want to cook themselves but don't have the time to shop..

Olashile is also a public speaker and she is passionate about helping people with open mind discover their purpose in life..

Olashile has won some reputable awards to her name like The Global Essence (The Republic of Ireland) award as the Young Achiever of the Year 2017, Unseen hero award 2017, The breaking barriers woman 2018, Lofty Heights Humanitarian award 2019, NHN one of the 50 Women in Leadership award 2019 amongst others..

Olashile Abayomi empowers women, she is very passionate about underprivileged women and she gives some of them Grants to start petty businesses .. She is well known in the local markets and under developed communities. She is also an advocate for non-violence against the BOY CHILD as well as for women.. She has helped victims get Justice overtime through her network and advocacy , something she does silently....

She is a God lover and is never ashamed of her Faith...
